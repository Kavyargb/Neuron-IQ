@Beginner
Gravity is an invisible pull. It is what keeps your feet on the ground and what makes apples fall from trees.
@Intermediate
Gravity is a fundamental interaction which causes mutual attraction between all things with mass or energy. It is what keeps the Earth in orbit around the Sun.
@Advanced
Gravity is most accurately described by the general theory of relativity, which describes gravity not as a force, but as a consequence of masses moving along geodesic lines in a curved spacetime.
