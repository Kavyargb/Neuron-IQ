Physics

Physics — Overview

Physics is the study of how the universe works. It explains why things fall, why stars shine, and how energy moves.

Deeper Dive

Physics is the natural science that studies matter, its fundamental constituents, its motion and behavior through space and time, and the related entities of energy and force.

Technical Details

Physics intersects with mathematics to formulate the standard model, quantum mechanics, and general relativity, mapping the deterministic and probabilistic nature of spacetime.
